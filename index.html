I am a Civil Engineer with experience in renewable energy project development, geospatial analysis, technical support, and monitoring/control systems for RES projects. In my current role, I support RES monitoring and control systems, helping diagnose connectivity, inverter/logger communication, Modbus, VPN, and field integration issues.

This portfolio presents selected work in GIS, renewable energy planning, data visualization, network analysis, and 3D design.

Renewable Energy Monitoring & Technical Support

Alongside my GIS and project development background, I have gained practical experience supporting renewable energy monitoring and control systems. My work involves diagnosing field issues, coordinating with technicians and vendors, reviewing system behaviour, and helping ensure that RES installations remain connected, monitored, and controllable.

  • Support for PV park monitoring and control systems.
  • Troubleshooting of connectivity, VPN, router, inverter, logger, and data communication issues.
  • Basic Modbus TCP/RTU checks, signal interpretation, and device integration support.
  • Coordination with clients, field technicians, vendors, and internal technical teams.
  • Preparation of technical notes, troubleshooting summaries, and installation guidance.
